Speedial

Dragging your bleep kicking and screaming into the 21st century.

Speedial lets you quickly dial extension numbers for the Norfolk & Norwich University Hospital.

Better communication = better care

Bleeps are horribly archaic. Speedial hopefully makes them slightly better.

Call from anywhere

No more hunting around waiting for a phone to become free. Extra bonus: answer from the comfort of your chair when bleeped in the canteen.

Get through quicker

Sidestep the bottleneck of Switchboard and reduce the incidence of Bleep & Run®.

Play safe, kids

Speedial is not a replacement for the internal phones.
It won't allow you to dial certain extensions like 2222. Signal coverage may also be patchy inside the N&N.

Speedial is not affiliated with the Norfolk and Norwich University Hospitals NHS Foundation Trust.
